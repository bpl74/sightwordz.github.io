FAQ

What is My Sight Wordz?

It is an iPhone app that helps children practice sight words through simple, repeatable routines and custom word lists.

It is designed for parents, caregivers, homeschool families, and early elementary readers.

Yes. You can create, import, and share custom word lists to match how your child is learning.

Yes. New users can go through an onboarding tour to quickly learn the home and word list screens.
