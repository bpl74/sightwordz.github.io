FAQ

Quick answers for App Store visitors

Can I add my child’s weekly classroom words?

Yes. Create your own lists and add the words you want your child to practice.

Does it have ads or in-app purchase traps?

No. The core experience is designed to stay clean and parent-first.

Does it track my child or collect personal data?

It is designed to minimize data and avoid tracking. Confirm the final details in your App Store privacy label.

Can I use the app without internet?

Yes. Practice sessions, custom lists, and progress tracking are built to work offline.

What if my child gets frustrated or tired?

That’s normal. MySightWordz is built for short, calm sessions. You can stop anytime and pick back up later.
